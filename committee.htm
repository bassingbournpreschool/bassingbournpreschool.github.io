Bassingbourn Pre-School Committee

Bassingbourn Pre-School is a committee-run Pre-School, meaning that responsibility for its safe and effective running is held jointly by the manager and the committee. Anyone is eligible to join the committee, provided they complete a DBS check for security purposes.
